About Us


Founded in 2020 and established through a process of fabric and material study, PISCES* examines the trends and patterns of modern,luxury wear inspired by nautical themes, class and modernity. PISCES* seeks to incite a progressive cultural conversation around the possibilities of modern luxury wear while staying true to the spirit of seafaring: elegance and excitement. PISCES*. Stay Anchored*.


PISCES* takes heavy inspiration from seafaring culture. We introduce the spirit of the sea to modern notions of beauty elegance.This careful, artisanal process gives our customers the products they love. PISCES* has gone beyond its nautical roots. Now, we integrate modernity and globalization into our designs. We have began expanding our palettes and utilizing fabric from sustainable sources in our products. In addition to existing brand case studies such as public exhibitions & runway shows, PISCES* will offer host of experiential events involving sound, image, film and fashion concepts.


"Stay anchored" - our motto comes from the intention of producing quality material fabric and products that should make the customer "anchored". We take pride not only in drawing back our customer to our products but creating an engaging culture with them.
